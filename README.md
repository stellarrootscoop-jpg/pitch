# Stellar Roots Global Cooperative
## Safe Land Stewardship — Pitch Deck & Prototype
This repository contains the pitch deck and prototype materials for Safe Land Stewardship a.k.a WEALTHFLOWER, a blockchain-powered platform developed by Stellar Roots Global Cooperative that connects regenerative land projects, community stewards, volunteers, and ethical investors across continents. The platform addresses the absence of trusted infrastructure for equitable land access by providing verified land listings, smart contract-backed agreements, contribution tracking, and transparent revenue sharing — designed specifically for economically and ecologically vulnerable communities. Stellar Roots is a living cooperative based in Amherst, Virginia, with existing land partnerships in the Carribbean, South East Asia, Africa and Europe, and is currently seeking seed funding and accelerator partnerships to bring the platform to full beta launch.
